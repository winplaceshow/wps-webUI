Find A Race

Goal

Win Place Show is an app used to predict the winner of the triple crown. Using Data taken from races in 2017 and 2018 we can predict the winner of each of the 2019 races. We cover each horse in all three triple crown races, The Kentucky Derby, Preakness Stakes, and Belmont Stakes. To get started just click on Find a Race or navigate to our login page using the menu in the top left.
